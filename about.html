About BioTrac

BioTrac is a social enterprise committed to transforming lives through education in health and financial literacy. We believe that informed individuals build resilient families and communities.

Our Vision

A world where every person — regardless of age or background — can access the knowledge they need to live healthy and financially stable lives.

Our Mission

To deliver relevant, practical, and culturally responsive training programs in schools, communities, and corporate environments.
